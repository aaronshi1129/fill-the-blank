Fill The Blank

Complete the sentence with the top 7 words Americans say, according to polls!

How to Play

1

Read the Sentence

You'll see a sentence with a blank space that needs to be filled.

2

Use the Initial Letters

Look at the initial letters provided as clues. Each letter represents the first letter of a possible answer.

3

Type Your Guesses

Enter words that could complete the sentence and start with the given letters.

4

Score Points

Each correct answer is worth 10 points. Find all 7 answers before the 60-second timer runs out!

5

Time Bonus

If you find all answers before time runs out, you'll get bonus points equal to the seconds remaining!

About the Game

Fill The Blank is a word game where you need to guess words that complete a sentence based on their initial letters. Each correct answer is worth 10 points.
